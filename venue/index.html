Traveling to Graz

  • Graz Airport (GRZ)
    • Local train S1 leaves every 30 minutes and takes about 13 minutes to the central station.
    • The other option is a taxi.
  • Vienna International Airport (VIE)
    • Roughly 3 hours by train or FlixBus from Vienna airport to Graz central station.
    • International airport with flights across Europe and worldwide.
  • Austrian Railways (ÖBB)
    • The central train station in Graz is here
    • Direct connections for instance to Budapest, Cologne, Innsbruck, Frankfurt, Linz, Ljubljana, Maribor, Munich, Münster, Prague, Trieste, Venice, Vienna, Zürich.
  • FlixBus
    • Direct connections for instance to Bratislava, Budapest, Cologne, Frankfurt, Ljubljana, Munich, Trieste, Venice, Vienna Airport, Zagreb.

Traveling within Graz

  • Graz Holding
    • Public transportation in Graz: trams (also called BIMs) and buses.
    • Google Maps gives good directions.
    • Ticket prices and information are given here.
    • It is possible to buy paper tickets from newspaper stands or the ticket machines within the bus or tram or at ticket machines at selected stops.
    • Instead of paper tickets, it is possible to use an app (Graz Mobil). (If you plan to use the app, please set it up in advance since it may take some time to get the paying method right.)
  • Bolt
    • Taxi service.

Getting to the IDea_Lab

  • Trams or buses going from Graz Central Station:
    • Tram number 1 to "Mariatrost", exit at "Tegetthoffplatz", 5 minutes walk
    • Tram number 7 to "LKH Med Uni", exit at "Merangasse", 8 minutes walk
    • Bus number 58 to "Ragnitz", exit at "Uni/Liebiggasse", 10 minutes walk
    • Bus number 63 to "Schulzentrum St. Peter", exit at "Graz Uni/Mensa (Leechgasse)", 5 minutes walk
  • Trams or buses going from Jakominiplatz (central transfer point):
    • Tram number 1 to "Mariatrost", exit at "Tegetthoffplatz", 5 minutes walk
    • Tram number 7 to "LKH Med Uni", exit at "Merangasse", 8 minutes walk
    • Bus number 31 to "UNI-Resowi", exit at "Graz Uni/Mensa (Schubertstraße)", 6 minutes walk
    • Bus number 39 to "Wirtschaftskammer", exit at "Zinzendorfgasse", 10 minutes walk
  • The main entrance of IDea_Lab is on the left side of the building.
