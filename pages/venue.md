# Traveling to Graz

- [Graz Airport (GRZ)](https://graz-airport.at/en/)
    - Local train S1 leaves every 30 minutes and takes about 13 minutes to the central station.
    - The other option is a taxi.
- [Vienna International Airport (VIE)](https://viennaairport.com/en/passengers)
    - Roughly 3 hours by train or FlixBus from Vienna airport to Graz central station.
    - International airport with flights across Europe and worldwide.
- [Austrian Railways (ÖBB)](https://www.oebb.at/en/)
    - The central train station in Graz is [here](https://maps.app.goo.gl/NXAUNWC3fVKprA3v5)
    - Direct connections for instance to Budapest, Cologne, Innsbruck, Frankfurt, Linz, Ljubljana, Maribor, Munich, Münster, Prague, Trieste, Venice, Vienna, Zürich.
- [FlixBus](https://global.flixbus.com/?_sp=db0ad30e-3f3f-4e6c-bdfb-707853b9340c.1775110601608&atb_pdid=801965b3-e0f5-43d7-9da7-3ae2f1ceeedc&noRedirect=true&_spnuid=16e8a380-a025-4071-b363-d72386307ebb_1775110601536)
    - Direct connections for instance to Bratislava, Budapest, Cologne, Frankfurt, Ljubljana, Munich, Trieste, Venice, Vienna Airport, Zagreb.

# Traveling within Graz

- [Graz Holding](https://www.holding-graz.at/en/mobility/)
    - Public transportation in Graz: trams (also called BIMs) and buses.
    - Google Maps gives good directions.
    - Ticket prices and information are given [here](https://www.holding-graz.at/en/mobility/ticketshop/).
    - It is possible to buy paper tickets from newspaper stands or the ticket machines within the bus or tram or at ticket machines at selected stops.
    - Instead of paper tickets, it is possible to use an [app (Graz Mobil)](https://www.holding-graz.at/en/mobility/ticketshop/).
      (If you plan to use the app, please set it up in advance since it may take some time to get the paying method right.)
- [Bolt](https://bolt.eu/de-de/cities/graz/)
    - Taxi service.
